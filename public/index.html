Rally for Unity, Change, and the Future of Our District

This isn’t just another rally—this is our moment of unity, our movement to restore balance to our district. It’s a chance to come together as a community and rally behind the leaders who truly represent us. Join us and feel the passion and commitment from candidates like Lt. Governor candidate Terry Goodin and US Congressional candidate Dr. Tim Peck, who are dedicated to fighting for the will of the people. Let the energy of former Indiana 9 Congressman Baron Hill, who served IN-9 with integrity and reflected our values, inspire you. And with Charlestown’s own Mayor Treva Hodges as MC, this event promises to be nothing short of uplifting. Bring your family for a day of fun—enjoy yard games, activities for the kids, and savor a famous Ribeye Sandwich from the Clark County Cattlemen. Plus, don’t miss your chance to win Taylor Swift tickets in our exciting concert raffle!

Get your own ‘Flip The District’ tee to wear to the rally here!

Let’s come together, rally for change, and shape the future for Hoosiers.
